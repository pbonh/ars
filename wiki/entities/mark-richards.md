## Overview

**Mark Richards** is a software architect and educator who has produced a video series on Architectural Decision Records. His lesson *"ADRs and Architecture Stories"* explains ADRs starting from Michael Nygard's original template and connects them to broader architecture communication practices.

## Characteristics

- **Educator**: Known for the *DeveloperToArchitect* learning platform and video series.
- **Template explainer**: Teaches Nygard's template as a foundation, then shows how to enrich it with narrative context.
- **Architecture-story advocate**: Links ADRs to the larger practice of telling "architecture stories" for stakeholder alignment.

## Common Strategies

- Teams new to ADRs can use Richards' video series as onboarding material for architects and senior developers.
- His framing of ADRs as part of "architecture stories" helps teams present decisions to non-technical stakeholders.
