## Overview

Manning Publications is an independent publisher of computer science and software engineering books. Known for the "in Action" and "in Depth" series, Manning produces technical titles across programming languages, frameworks, machine learning, and infrastructure. Their publications are available in print, eBook, and through the liveBook online reader.

## Characteristics

- **Independent publisher**: Focused on practitioner-oriented content rather than academic textbooks.
- **MEAP program**: Manning Early Access Program allows readers to purchase and read chapters as they are written.
- **liveBook platform**: Interactive online reading with search, code execution, and community discussion.
- **Broad catalog**: Covers JavaScript/TypeScript, Java, Python, .NET, Rust, Go, cloud-native technologies, and AI/ML.

## Common Strategies

- Using Manning titles for structured, project-based learning in professional software development.
- Leveraging MEAP access to stay current with evolving technologies (e.g., new TypeScript or framework releases).
