## Overview

Nous Research is the AI research lab behind Hermes Agent, as well as the Hermes, Nomos, and Psyche model families. They built Hermes Agent as a research-ready autonomous agent platform with features for batch processing, trajectory export, and RL training integration.

## Characteristics

- **Model training focus**: Built by model trainers, not just application developers.
- **Model ecosystem**: Hermes, Nomos, and Psyche models.
- **Platform integration**: Works with Nous Portal, OpenRouter, OpenAI, or any compatible endpoint.
- **Research features**: Batch trajectory generation, ShareGPT-format export, RL training with Atropos.

## Common Strategies

- **Use Nous Portal**: Native integration for model access and management.
- **OpenRouter routing**: Use OpenRouter as a primary provider with Nous models.
- **Research data collection**: Export agent trajectories for training data generation.
