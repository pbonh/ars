## Overview

The **ADR GitHub Organization** (`github.com/adr`) is a community-driven group that maintains the [adr.github.io](https://adr.github.io) website and related repositories. Its mission is to promote the capture of architectural decisions, provide a common vocabulary, and strengthen the tooling ecosystem around ADRs.

## Characteristics

- **Community hub**: Curates templates, tools, and educational resources for Architectural Decision Records.
- **Vendor-neutral**: Not tied to a single company or framework; references multiple industry sources.
- **Active curation**: Maintains lists of ADR templates, decision-capturing tools, and media coverage.

## Common Strategies

- Teams use the organization's website to select an ADR template that fits their team's culture and compliance needs.
- The site's decision-capturing tools list helps evaluate lightweight CLI tools, IDE plugins, and documentation generators.
- The media and research pointers are useful for justifying ADR adoption to stakeholders or in architecture review boards.
