## Overview

PaCoRe (Parallel Coordinated Reasoning) is a framework developed by StepFun-AI that demonstrates heavy thinking by decomposing reasoning into two distinct stages: parallel reasoning followed by sequential deliberation. It is cited in the Heavy Skill paper as an alternative approach alongside Kimi K2 and LongCat-Flash-Thinking-2601.

## Characteristics

- **Developer**: StepFun-AI
- **Approach**: Learning to scale test-time compute with parallel coordinated reasoning
- **Availability**: Open-source framework referenced at https://github.com/stepfun-ai/PaCoRe
- **Relationship to Heavy Skill**: Provides independent evidence that the two-stage heavy thinking pattern generalizes across different research groups and implementations

## Common Strategies

- Used as a comparison point for heavy thinking implementations
- Demonstrates that the parallel-then-deliberate pattern is emerging across the LLM reasoning landscape
- Referenced as prior work showing promising results for test-time scaling via stage decomposition
