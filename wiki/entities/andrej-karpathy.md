## Overview

Andrej Karpathy is an AI researcher, educator, and software engineer known for influential work in deep learning, computer vision, and large language model applications. He was a founding member of OpenAI, led the computer vision team at Tesla Autopilot, and served as Director of AI at Tesla. He is widely recognized for making complex AI concepts accessible through blog posts, videos, and open-source tools.

## Characteristics

- **Research focus:** Deep learning architectures, reinforcement learning, computer vision, LLM agent design.
- **Communication style:** Clear, minimal-jargon explanations with strong intuitive analogies.
- **Tool building:** Creates practical patterns and tools (e.g., the LLM Wiki pattern, nanoGPT) rather than purely theoretical work.
- **Current activity:** Independent research, education, and advisory roles.

## Common Strategies

- **LLM Wikis for personal knowledge management:** Karathy advocates using an LLM agent to maintain a persistent markdown wiki, with the human curating sources and the LLM handling cross-referencing and maintenance. He runs the agent alongside Obsidian for real-time browsing.
- **Agent-as-programmer pattern:** Treats the LLM as a programmer, the wiki as a codebase, and the markdown viewer as an IDE.
- **Concrete over abstract:** Prefers to communicate ideas through working code, patterns, and minimal viable examples rather than formal frameworks.
